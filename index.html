About JoviGroove

This section should make people feel your identity fast: a skilled rhythm artist with stage energy, teaching ability, and a real creative presence.

Rhythm that moves people

JoviGroove is the artist portfolio of Jovi — a drummer, street dance performer, and rhythm creative based in the Ottawa–Gatineau region. From live playing to workshops and creative collaborations, the mission is simple: bring movement, groove, precision, and life to every room.

Whether performing on stage, teaching students, or building strong artistic experiences, Jovi brings a mix of musicality, discipline, and expressive energy that people remember.
